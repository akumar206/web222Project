About Football

Football, also known as soccer in some parts of the world, is a popular team sport played with a round ball between two teams of eleven players. The objective of the game is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opposing team's goal.


Football Rules

Football has a set of rules that govern how the game is played. Some of the key rules include:

  • The ball must be kicked, not touched with the hands or arms (with a few exceptions for the goalkeeper).
  • Players must not use excessive force or make dangerous tackles.
  • The game is divided into two 45-minute halves with a 15-minute halftime break.
